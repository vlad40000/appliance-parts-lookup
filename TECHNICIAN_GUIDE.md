# Appliance Parts Lookup - Technician Guide

## Overview

The **Appliance Parts Lookup** application is a mobile-responsive tool designed to help appliance technicians quickly find parts, assembly diagrams, and pricing information for appliance models. The app supports three input methods: manual model entry, image upload with OCR, and live camera capture.

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
2. Navigate to your Manus-hosted application URL
3. The home page displays three input tabs: **Manual**, **Upload**, and **Camera**

---

## Method 1: Manual Model Entry

### Steps

1. Click the **Manual** tab on the home page
2. Enter the appliance model number in the text field (e.g., `MVWB300WQ1`)
3. Click the **Search** button
4. Wait for results to load (typically 2-5 seconds)

### Tips

- Model numbers are typically found on a label inside the appliance door or on the back panel
- Enter the complete model number for best results
- The search is case-insensitive

---

## Method 2: Image Upload with OCR

### Steps

1. Click the **Upload** tab on the home page
2. Either:
   - **Drag and drop** an image of the model label onto the upload area, or
   - **Click to browse** and select an image file from your device
3. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP (max 5MB)
4. The app automatically extracts the model number using AI vision
5. Once extracted, the lookup begins automatically

### Best Practices

- Photograph the model label clearly with good lighting
- Ensure the entire model number is visible in the frame
- Avoid shadows or glare on the label
- Use a clean, flat angle (not at an extreme angle)

### Supported Image Formats

- PNG (recommended for clarity)
- JPG/JPEG
- WebP
- Maximum file size: 5MB

---

## Method 3: Live Camera Capture (Mobile)

### Steps

1. On a mobile device, click the **Camera** tab
2. Grant camera permissions when prompted
3. Point the camera at the model label
4. Click the **Capture** button to take a photo
5. The app automatically extracts the model number and begins the lookup

### Requirements

- Mobile device with a working camera
- Browser must have camera permission enabled
- Good lighting on the model label

### Tips

- Hold the device steady for a clear photo
- Ensure the entire model number is in frame
- Use natural lighting when possible

---

## Reading the Results

### Diagram Sections

Once a model is found, the results page displays **diagram sections** as thumbnail cards:

- **Top & Cabinet** - Cabinet structure and top components
- **Control Panel** - Buttons, dials, and electronic controls
- **Motor/Basket/Tub** - Internal motor and drum assembly
- **Pump** - Drain pump and water circulation
- **Optional Parts** - Accessories and upgrades

### Viewing Full Diagrams

1. Click any diagram thumbnail to open a full-size viewer
2. Use the **Zoom In** (+) and **Zoom Out** (-) buttons to adjust magnification
3. The zoom level is displayed as a percentage (50% - 300%)
4. Click outside the diagram or press the back button to close

### Parts Lists

Each diagram section contains an expandable parts list:

1. Click the section name to expand/collapse the parts list
2. Each part shows:
   - **Item #** - Reference number in the diagram
   - **Part #** - Manufacturer part number
   - **Description** - Component name and details
   - **Price** - Current retail price (if available)

---

## Searching and Filtering Parts

### Quick Search

1. Use the search box at the top of the results page
2. Type any keyword: part number, description, or item number
3. Results update in real-time as you type
4. Matching parts are highlighted across all sections

### Example Searches

- Search by **part number**: `W10671140` (finds exact part)
- Search by **description**: `pump` (finds all pump-related parts)
- Search by **item number**: `12` (finds parts labeled as item 12)

---

## Using Search History

### Accessing Recent Searches

The app automatically tracks your lookup history during your session:

1. On the home page, your recent searches appear below the input tabs
2. Click any recent model to quickly re-access its diagrams and parts
3. History is saved per user session

### Clearing History

History is automatically cleared when you close the browser or log out.

## Tips for Field Use

### Mobile Optimization

- The app is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops
- On mobile, use landscape orientation for better diagram viewing
- Camera capture is fastest for field work

### Performance

- Diagrams are cached for faster repeat lookups
- The app works offline for previously viewed models (limited functionality)
- Typical lookup time: 2-5 seconds on standard internet

### Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Model not found | Verify the model number is complete and correct |
| Image upload fails | Ensure image is clear, under 5MB, and in a supported format |
| Camera won't open | Check browser permissions and try a different browser |
| Diagrams load slowly | Check internet connection; try refreshing the page |
| OCR extraction incorrect | Try a clearer photo or use manual entry instead |
